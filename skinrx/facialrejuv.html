A slightly aggressive procedure, not for the faint at heart. But then again a little sacrifice won’t hurt right? Known to the Medical World as Collagen Induction therapy, this procedure involves repeatedly puncturing the skin with tiny, sterile needles (microneedling the skin) in order to induce endogenous production of cutaneous collagen. An automated microneedle stamping device, or "microneedling pen", powered by a motor and adjusted for frequency (stamps/second) and depth of the microneedles is used. This clinic procedure is solely and specifically done by our board certified dermatologist under topical anesthesia. Sited as the next big thing, microneedling thus address a lot of skin issues. A new treatment modality for the treatment of scars, especially acne scars, stretch marks, wrinkles, and for facial rejuvenation as well.

Botox injections should be given only by a trained and knowledgeable medical professional, even when used for cosmetic purposes. By this, We assure you that our Dermatologists are skilled and highly qualified to administer botox injections.
