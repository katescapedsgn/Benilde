about us

history

Concepted by three good friends, amateur in business but all board certified, trained dermatologists. We aim to deliver extensive yet affordable skin care services, with exemplary quality and patient care.

Back in 2011, the business was called skinclinique. It was located at Broadway Centrum, Aurora Boulevard, Quezon City. However, they needed a bigger space for the increasing number of people coming in. That is why in 2015, they opened their new store in Nia Road, Barangay Pinahan, Quezon City.

They renamed their business to Skin Rx Face & Body Wellness. Since then, it still has been giving affordable and efficient skin care service to both men and women. Truly one of the best.

company

We are dedicated to provide superior medical treatment for a broad spectrum of dermatological conditions.

Our practice consists of board certified dermatologists who underwent extensive training and experience, allowing them to provide cost effective, efficient care for disorders and conditions of the skin.

Therefore, the main purpose is to return the dermis to its original condition: beautiful and youthful in nature.
